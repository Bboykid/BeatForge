# You Are Beautiful — 采样想法

James Blunt — You're Beautiful 的人声采样方案，适用于 Memphis Typebeat / Dark Plugg / Chopped & Screwed。

---

## 原曲信息

- **BPM**: ~120
- **Key**: E Major
- **适合拉到**: 60-65 BPM（降半速）

---

## 可用采样素材

原歌词中适合切片的片段：

| 片段 | 特点 |
|---|---|
| "beautiful..." | 尾音拖长，氛围感强 |
| "angel" | 词尾有共鸣，适合做 pad |
| "saw your face" | 节奏感好，可做 hook |
| "crowded place" | 尾音 open，适合加 reverb |
| "never be with you" | 情绪最浓的一句 |
| "flying high" | 词尾 high 可单独切 |
| "she smiled at me" | 叙事感好，适合 verse |
| "another man" | 三个音节节奏清晰 |
| "got a plan" | 干脆利落，适合做 tag |
| "la-la-la..." (outro) | 可直接当旋律 loop |

---

## 重组方案 A — 偏执感（经典 Memphis 黑暗叙事）

```
[Verse]
"I saw an angel... on the subway..."
"she was with another man..."

[Chop / Hook]
"beautiful... beautiful..."
"but I'll never... never... never be with you"

[Bridge]
"I saw your face in a crowded place"
"she smiled at me... but she was with another man"
```

## 重组方案 B — 极简重复

只挑 3-4 个词反复切，最有 Memphis 味：

```
"another man... another man... another man..."
"but I... but I... but I... got a plan..."

然后炸出来:
"YOU'RE BEAUTIFUL"
```

## 重组方案 C — 时空错乱感（Chopped & Screwed）

打乱时间线：

```
"she smiled at me..."
"we shared a moment..."
"I saw an angel..."
(停顿)
"never be with you"
```

## 重组方案 D — 禁忌叙事

```
"I saw an angel... flying high..."
"she was with another man... but I got a plan..."

(第二段降半速)
"I s-a-w y-o-u-r f-a-c-e..."

"NEVER... BE... WITH... YOU"
```

---

## FL Studio 操作步骤

1. 把人声拖进 **Edison**
2. `Ctrl+E` 打开切片工具，标记感兴趣的词
3. 把切片拖到 **Playlist**，按方案排列
4. 每个切片右键 **Pitch shift** 降 3-5 semitones
5. 关键位置加 **Gross Beat** 半速效果
6. 加 **Reverb send** 甩几个词到背景
7. 铺 808 + 硬 snare + 三连音 hi-hat
